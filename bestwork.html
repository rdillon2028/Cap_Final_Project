In my opinion, the unit 5 recommendation app demonstrates my best work. I felt like these questions narrowed down the final answer and were good questions to ask. The most difficult part about this project was tallying up the final answer. I had to take all of my data from when my friends and family took my quiz and I had to create challenging functions and it was very confusing at first. In order to create this work, I had to keep all of my code very organized otherwise I would forget where I was at in my coding and have to start all over again. Overall, I am most impressed with my unit 5 recommendation app because it showcases my creative talent.
